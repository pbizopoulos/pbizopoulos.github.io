Paschalis Bizopoulos

Summary

Paschalis Bizopoulos (in Greek: Πασχάλης Μπιζόπουλος) is a computer engineer with experience in designing, developing, deploying, and maintaining reproducible AI systems using Nix-based environments, portable tooling, and DevOps/MLOps best practices. He proposed Sparsely Activated Networks [pdf] [code] [web application], a method for signal decomposition, compression and pattern discovery.
