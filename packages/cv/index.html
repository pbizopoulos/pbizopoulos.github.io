Summary

Paschalis Bizopoulos (in Greek: Πασχάλης Μπιζόπουλος) is a computer engineer with experience in designing, developing, deploying, and maintaining Nix-based AI applications using portable tools, adhering to well-established standards, and applying best practices in DevOps and MLOps. He invented Sparsely activated networks, which can be used for signal decomposition, compression and motif finding.
