Paschalis Bizopoulos has a BSc in Electrical and Computer Engineering from ECE-AUTH (2010), a MSc in Wireless Communication Systems from Brunel University (2012) and a PhD in Machine Learning from ECE-NTUA (2019). He was also a Fulbright Doctoral Dissertation Visiting Research Student at Harvard-MIT Biomedical Center (Fall 2017). He invented Sparsely Activated Networks. His research interests are artificial intelligence and neural networks.
